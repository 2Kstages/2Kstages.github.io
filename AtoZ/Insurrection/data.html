Insurrection

In a dark tale of honour and redemption, a vast fleet of SJS marines (think Warhammer 40K Space Marines in Judge helmets) threaten an outpost that has declared independence.

This galaxy-spanning Dreddverse tale explores the idea of Mega-City One's wider off-Earth concerns (previously visited in The Corps and Maelstrom) before providing a gossamer link to the later Lawless.

Art by Colin MacNeil
