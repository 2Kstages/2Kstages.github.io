Valkyries

Evidence that Space Girls was actually quite subtle is provided here with a male gaze notion of what it would be like in the far future if Norse mythology was matriarchal instead of patriarchal. Valhalla forfend that this would just be an excuse to have a bunch of buxom, mostly nude nymphomaniacs parade across the page for six weeks.

Story-wise: minor royalty has been kidnapped and must be rescued by the Valkyries, which necessitates that they take off most of their clothes and then cuddle afterwards.

Art by John Lucas & Len O'Grady
