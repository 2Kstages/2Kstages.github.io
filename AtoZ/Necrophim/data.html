Necrophim

Intrigue and betrayal lead to a war fought in Hell between Satan and the other denizens. The strong art style and interesting design aesthetic has Satan presented as an egotistical, unbalanced rock-goth lothario suffering from chronic ennui.

Art by Lee Carter
