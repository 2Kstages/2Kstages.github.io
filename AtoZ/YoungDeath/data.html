Young Death

Boyhood of a Superfiend gave Judge Death an ego and a backstory (and introduced us to Mrs. Gunderson), and has become the groundwork for later flashback depictions of his world, both in Anderson's Half-Life and in the modern Fall of Deadworld.

Art by Peter Doherty
