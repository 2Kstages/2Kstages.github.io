[Mechanismo]

The Mechanismo program is the long-running and sometimes disastrous attempt to use robot Judges in Mega-City One. Machine Law saw Mark 8 robot Judges enter active service under Chief Judge Logan.

Specific robot Judges not included in this listing are Judge Estabon of Department K and Judge Patsy (of the Patsy & Wrexler partnership).

Art by Dan Cornwell, Colin MacNeil & Chris Blythe
