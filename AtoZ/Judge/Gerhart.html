Judge Gerhart

SJS Judge Alex Gerhart was determined to find good reason to question Dredd's judgement following Chaos Day, but ultimately they became close allies against deep cover operative Judge Smiley. Gerhart was so badly wounded in the line of duty that much of his original body was replaced by cybernetic implants.

Art by Chris Weston & Dylan Teague
