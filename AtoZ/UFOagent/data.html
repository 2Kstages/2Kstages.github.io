U.F.O. Agent / Smokeman

A reprinting of an old Eagle series, that here provided some content for the early Dan Dare and 2000 AD Annuals. Human agents work for some aliens, but it plays out low-key: like Harry Palmer's having an odd dream.

Art by Jose Ortiz
