Hook Jaw

Clearly inspired by Jaws, except that the shark has a giant harpoon stuck in its maw. Originally a strip in the 1976 comic Action, the character crossed over into 2000 AD for a soft reboot series with a more supernatural flavour.

A Titan Comics' 2016 reboot is reprinted in the Megazine in 2024.

Art by Leigh Gallagher
