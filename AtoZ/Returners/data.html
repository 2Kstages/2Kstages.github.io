The Returners

Set initially in Ciudad Barranquilla, four misfits mysteriously come back to life after violent deaths, leading to a series of occult, globe-trotting adventures.

The fourth book is oddly titled, as it takes place in the jungle outside of Ciudad Barranquilla, but is named Amazonia.

Art by Nicolo Assirelli & Eva de la Cruz
