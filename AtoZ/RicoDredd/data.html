[Rico Dredd]

Dredd's infamous clone brother, tainted by corruption and ultimately sent to the penal colony of Titan. The younger, not yet entirely corrupt version co-stars in the canon-lite Judge Cadet Dredd. This Rico is not to be confused with younger Dredd clone Judge Rico.

Art by Paul Johnson
