One-Eyed Jack

Jack McBane is a hard-bitten cop in 70's New York. Originally published as a series in Valiant and served as a creative precursor to Judge Dredd. The story One-Eyed Jacks ties the two together.

Art by Kieran McKeown & Quinton Winter
