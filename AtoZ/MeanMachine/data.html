The Mean Machine

A member of the Angel Gang so popular (with the readership, that is) that despite him losing an arm and being killed, he was resurrected for many more headbutting adventures of limited eleoquence and carefully graded rage. The dial on his head has four settings, y'see: (1) surly, (2) brutal, (3) vicious and (4) downright mean: pray it don't get stuck on four and a half!

Art by Nick Percival
