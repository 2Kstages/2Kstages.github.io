Fink Angel

When Dredd defeated the Angel Gang during the quest to retrieve the Judge Child, little did he know that there was a fifth, Earth-bound member of the gang that would later seek vengeance. Fink, a cadaverous monster, lives in the ground with his vicious pet Ratty, and is an expert in various pizens, used to dispatch or incapacitate his victims.

Art by Mike McMahon
