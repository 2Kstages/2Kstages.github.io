[Deadworld Designs]

Callum and Moira, Calhab immigrants to Mega-City One, run Deadworld Designs (more or less a toy shop) - but just as a front. Their ultimate plan is to bring back the Dark Judges.

Art by Neil Googe & Gary Caldwell
