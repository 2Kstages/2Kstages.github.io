Young Middenface McNulty

Later shortened to Young Middenface, this is sometimes out and out comedy, sometimes drama and always a little bit shortbread tin (as with Calhab Justice) - never afraid to throw on some tartan and utter a "crivens". These are tales set prior to Middenface's appearance in the Strontium Dog origin story Portrait of a Mutant.

Art by Shaun Thomas
