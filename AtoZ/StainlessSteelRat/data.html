The Stainless Steel Rat

James Bolivar "Slippery Jim" DiGriz is the titular rat: an interstellar rogue that justifies (non-homicidal) theft on the basis that it's covered by (someone's) insurance. Carlos Ezquerra beautifully realizes these serialized versions of Harry Harrison's space operatic novels.

Art by Carlos Ezquerra
