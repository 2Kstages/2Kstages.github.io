The Corps

Space Judges versus a Sino-Klegg alliance! The deeper story is one of dealing with brutalized squad members who feel free to murder out of hand. The idea of a wider space-going arm of the Justice Department gets rekindled in Maelstrom and can be seen as an early inspiration for the modern classic Insurrection.

Art by Colin MacNeil, Paul Marshall & Dondie Cox
