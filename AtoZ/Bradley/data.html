Bradley

Bradley (a troublesome alien child) and his friends Milton (giant psychopathic child that all adults and most children are afraid of) and Annabella (who uses her smarts and chutzpah to pacify Milton) get in trouble, go in search of some great music to enlighten an older relative and finish out by starring in some variations on classic fairy tales.

Art by Simon Harrison
