Ordinary

Instead of a zombie apocalypse, here we get a superhero pandemic: everyone on the planet suddenly develops superpowers (with predictably disastrous results) - except for Michael Fisher, who's just too ordinary. Really, this is a story of hope for someone who can't even get a break in his fantasies.

Art by D'Israeli
