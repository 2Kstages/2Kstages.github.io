Max Normal

Max Normal, the Pinstripe Freak is an odd blend of Huggy Bear, Jack Kerouac, Cary Grant & Clark Gable's moustache, who serves as a hip-talking informer for Dredd. Arguably best used in the intended role of a supporting character, Max has recently been given his own series. How much you love the Marmite depends on your ability to withstand the rapid fire chitter chatter: it's not light, it's not a smatter but rather the latter. So you might lose sight of your drive to thrive, baby!

Art by Jon Davis-Hunt
