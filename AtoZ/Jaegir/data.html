Jaegir

Just when you thought it was safe to take off the chem mask, Kapiten-Inspector Atalia Jaegir of the Nordland State Security Police suddenly kicks back open the door to the Rogue-verse with darkly atmospheric tales of betrayal, vengeance and a desperate search for some kind of redemption.

Art by Simon Coleby & Len O'Grady
