Counterfeit Girl

This cross between Johnny Mnemonic and 1990's Shadows is a dystopian cyberpunk melange that, like Blade Runner, forces the protagonist to question their own sense of self (and has scene-stealing billboards).

Art by Rufus Dayglo & Dom Regan
