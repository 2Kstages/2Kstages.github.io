Babe Race 2000

Fairly obvious from the title, but this is like Death Race 2000 but they've replaced all the drivers with heavily-armed strip club babes. It steals from Viz's Millie Tant by using the threadbare mysoginistic reasoning that anyone that complains about the sexualization of women is a fat, ugly lesbian. Needlessly offensive claptrap dressed up as a mean-spirited Whacky Races.

Art by Anthony Williams
