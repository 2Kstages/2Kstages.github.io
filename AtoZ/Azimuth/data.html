Azimuth

A stealth thrill that's part of Sinister Dexter continuity. This caused some controversy as a portion of the readership were disappointed that the new character of Suzi Nine and the mysterious world of Azimuth morphed into an existing property. The art hid at least one QR code that foreshadowed later developments.

Art by Tazio Bettin
