Soul Gun Warrior

The soul gun uses as bullets the souls of pilots who have to commit suicide. So, the ghost of a US operative fights the ghost of Uri Gagarin, who is sabotaging space shuttles. It's Rocky IV meets pop art pulp sci-fi.

Art by Shaky Kane
