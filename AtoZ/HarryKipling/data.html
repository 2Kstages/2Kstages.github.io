Harry Kipling (Deceased)

Three years before chatty zombie Zombo, there was this other chatty zombie: Harry Kipling. Even though he's a partly cybernetic zombie on an interplanetary crusade to assassinate gods, he's also a throwback to a militarized British Empire - what what? Tally ho!

Art by Boo Cook
