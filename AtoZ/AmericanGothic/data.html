American Gothic

Europe's mythological humanoids, driven out of the old country by prejudice, seek freedom in the new world, and are part of a wild west wagon train crossing the north american continent.

Edginton's "Wheel of Worlds" multiverse manages to link this world to that of Leviathan through the sharing of some of Captain McLean's Old Rotgut.

Art by Mike Collins
