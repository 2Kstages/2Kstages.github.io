Visions of Deadworld

This third part of the historical Deadworld sequence spins away from the narrative left dangling at the end of The Fall of Deadworld to drift into the past and the future, rounding out character beats but in some ways treading water.

Mention of a triad consuming the magnetosphere of the planet in the distant future speaks to the idea of the Sisters of Death as a group of three, linking them to the mythological Furies (explored in various forms, such as the three witches of Macbeth, the Kindly Ones of The Sandman or the triple aspect goddess Danu in Slaine).

Art by Dave Kendall
