Brigand Doom

It's Z for Vendetta, as a motiveless, murderous and unbeatable drug-addicted zombie-thug in a Dick Turpin outfit has weird eyes in a bleak dystopian metropolis. Investigator 9 is stalked by him and responds by leaning heavily against doorframes filled with ennui.

Art by Dave D'Antiquis
