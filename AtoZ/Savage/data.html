Savage

A direct sequel to Invasion (really, a continuation of the narrative), although unexpected as it cropped up twenty-six years later. Bill Savage is a no-nonsense resistance fighter taking on a Russian Volgan invasion of Britain.

In a bit of a Millsversian coup, the origins of several A.B.C. Warriors are woven into the tapestry, along with a backstory for Mr. Ten Percent of Ro-Busters.

Art by Patrick Goddard
