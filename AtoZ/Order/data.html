The Order

In medieval Europe a young warrior woman seeks information on her missing father, only to discover his old friends are a gang of ancient, time-traveling adventurers whose task is to stop evil worm-things from invading Earth from another dimension.

Subsequent series fold in a wide variety of historical figures at a fair clip (so keep your references handy) and a retconned Armoured Gideon gets looped in from the fourth series onwards.

Art by John Burns
