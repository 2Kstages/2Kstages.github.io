Surfer

Zane Perks is a talented sky surfer whose hero is the famous Marlon "Chopper" Shakespeare. Down on his luck, he jumps at an opportunity to work as a stunt double of his hero for a holo-vid recreation of Supersurf 7.

Art by Colin MacNeil & Chris Blythe
