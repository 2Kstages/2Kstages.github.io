13 Thirteen

In what seems like some kind of wish fulfilment shangri-la, a telekinetic chancer finds a magic pearl that gives him superpowers with which to save the Earth, and is helped on his quest by a mostly naked, sword-wielding super-model.

Art by Andy Clarke
