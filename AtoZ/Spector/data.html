Spector

A wise-cracking robo-detective finds himself sandwiched between two enemies: organized crime and the corrupt police force that supports it. Stylistically, it's a light-hearted mash-up of hard-boiled noir and Robocop.

The first two episodes were drawn by Carlos Ezquerra and published posthumously in 2019. These were then reprinted in 2023, with the series being completed by Dan Cornwell & Dylan Teague.

Art by Carlos Ezquerra
