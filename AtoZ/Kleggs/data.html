The Kleggs

Ravening reptilian humanoids with a penchant for human flesh, we first met the Kleggs as they were hired as mercenaries by the mad Chief Judge Cal. Although not listed here, the Kleggs also show up in Ace Trucking Co. (suggesting, rather loosely, that perhaps that story takes place in the Dreddverse). Sensitive Klegg is a pacifist that doesn't have the stereotypical characteristics of the other members of his race that we've met.

Art by Brian Bolland
