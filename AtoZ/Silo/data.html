Silo

Perhaps inspired by scenes in movies such as WarGames and Damnation Alley featuring the two-man rule of nuclear launch protocol and definitely borrowing heavily from both The Shining and Die Hard, this is tonally a blend of 70s and 80s horror movies: dark, bloody and ultimately somewhat confusing.

Art by Dave D'Antiquis
