Return of the Taxidermist

Jacob Sardini is a human taxidermist and where the first tale is very much Mega-City One meets The Godfather, the second is more of a shaggy dog comedy where he competes in the Mega-Olympics alongside future sports such as Olympic Staring, and Olympic Sex.

Art by Ian Gibson
