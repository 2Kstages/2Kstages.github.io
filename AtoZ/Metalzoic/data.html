Metalzoic

The story (set on a future Earth where machines have evolved into sentience and are reminsicent of extinct mammalian life) is complex but revolves around a conflict between two tribes: the wheeldebeasts led by Amok and the Mekaka led by Armageddon.

Art by Kevin O'Neill
