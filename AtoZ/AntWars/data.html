Ant Wars

Them!: giant ants meet humans, with predictable results. The real joy here (apart from watching people being torn apart by giant ants) is that the indigenous character keeps getting dismissed out of hand when actually he has the best ideas for avoiding being torn apart. Listen to him, ya schmucks!

The other listed stories are thematic, rather than obvious narrative additions.

Art by Jose Luis Ferrer
