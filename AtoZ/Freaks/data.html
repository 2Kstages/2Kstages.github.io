Freaks

An attractive yet shallow and selfish yuppie finds himself transported to the world of the Kakkaks, where he's the ugly, weird one. Him being the alien, the locals want to harvest him for military secrets and biological study. Can he find a way home, and does he deserve one?

Art by John Higgins
