Disaster 1990

A prequel to Invasion, in which an apocalyptic flood (which has entirely receded nine years later) causes a Lord of the Flies situation across lowland Britain.

As this stars Bill Savage, we could argue that it deserves to be included in the Millsverse, but there are no other elements of his work present here, and with the different writer it's probably okay to treat this as a pseudo-canon curio.

Art by Carlos Pino
