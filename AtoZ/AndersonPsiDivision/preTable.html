Anderson Psi Division

Psi-Judge Cassandra Anderson introduced us to the Psi-Division of the Mega-City One Justice Department, as she battled the other-dimensional menace of Judge Death. Her early, breezy humour provided a counterpoint to Dredd's taciturnity. Branching out on her own she's variously dealt with psychic threats, resurgences from the Dark Judges and an ongoing feud with Orlok the Assassin. She's even been known to go backpacking around the galaxy to find herself.
