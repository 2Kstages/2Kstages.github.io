Calhab Justice

A Scottish Dreddverse tale that struggles to marry well the conspiracy drama of an impending nuclear apocalypse with someone in a tam o' shanter saying "crivvens". The shortbread tin dressings are somewhat shelved as the series goes on to focus more on the dark tragedy surrounding a murderous empath.

Art by John Ridgway
