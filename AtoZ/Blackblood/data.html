Blackblood

Blackblood was constructed as a Russian Volgan war droid specializing in brutality and treachery, and is a main character in The A.B.C. Warriors. His prototype model can also be seen in some episodes of Savage, but here he gets a couple of stories under his own banner.

Art by Kevin Walker
