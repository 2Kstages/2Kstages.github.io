Chronos Carnival

There's this theme park inside a (big) spaceship, and sometimes dangerous triangular portals to other worlds open up inside some of the attractions, so the staff are also heavily armed (in case anything bad pops through), and adventurous. It's like Scooby Doo, but with laser guns.

Art by Ron Smith
