America

A seminal tale of the struggle between democracy and authoritarianism in Mega-City One. America Jara is the daughter of Porta Rican immigrants, and grows up friends with Bennett Beeny. As she joins a pro-democracy movement and becomes involved with their militant wing (Total War), Beeny becomes a famous singer and their lives drift apart: until a crisis reunites them in tragedy.

Art by Colin MacNeil
