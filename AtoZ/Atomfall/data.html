Atomfall

In an alternate post-war Britain, the Windscale Atom Plant suffers a mysterious accident that requires containment. Unfortunately, it's not really a complete story as it only takes you so far into a second act before revealing itself as a sort of long-form teaser ad for the game of the same name.

Art by Anthony Williams & Steve Canon
