Dead Eyes

Eventually revealing itself as a stealth thrill leading us back into the Indigo Prime multiverse, this starts out as a pscyhological thriller in which squaddie Danny Redman, having narrowly survived combat in Iraq, finds himself being experimented on in a secret British government facility, and determines that he must escape.

Art by Lee Carter
