Major Eazy

Major Eazy is an authority-phobic GI battling through war-torn Europe during World War II. Originally a strip in Battle (created by Alan Hebden and Carlos Ezquerra), this has resurfaced as part of Rebellion's Treasury of British Comics stable.

The character's laconic demeanour was based on James Coburn and later repurposed for the Megazine's Cursed Earth Koburn.

Art by Dan Cornwell & Dylan Teague
