Middenface McNulty

Sometimes a comedy character, Middenface is a Scottish Strontium Dog who often works alongside Johnny Alpha, and met him during the first mutant uprising depicted in Portrait of a Mutant. Tales of McNulty's adventures prior to this are told under the banner of Young Middenface.

Art by Carlos Ezquerra
